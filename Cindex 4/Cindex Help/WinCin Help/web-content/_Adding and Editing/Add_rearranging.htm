Rearranging text

Flipping fields

Cindex provides two ways to exchange the contents of a heading and the subheading (or locator) following it.

  1. When you have a record open for editing, place the insertion point anywhere in the upper field and hit CONTROL +. To pull up a heading to replace the one at the level above (half flip) place the insertion point anywhere in the upper field and hit CONTROL –. Cindex also provides toolbar buttons for executing full- and half-flips.
  2. When you are working in the main index view, Cindex can exchange the contents of each main heading and the field following it (the first subheading if there is one, otherwise the locator field) in a single operation on multiple records. Select the relevant records, then choose Flip Headings (Edit menu), or choose from the contextual menu.

Cindex provides a powerful 'smart' flip, that automatically exchanges the capitalization of headings, and moves prepositions and articles (or, in the case of See also references placed in the locator field, the prefix phrase) when it flips the fields.

To enable or disable smart flipping, choose Preferences… (Edit menu) and on the Editing tab check Smart Flip. To specify the articles and prepositions that can be moved during a flip, choose Smart Flip Words… from the Document menu. If you place a ~ immediately before any word in the Smart Flip set, Cindex will move that word from one field to the other when flipping, but will not move it from the beginning to the end of the field, or vice-versa. Words for which you would typically want this behavior are "and" and "versus."

When you use a keyboard shortcut to make a full flip (CONTROL +) or a half flip (CONTROL –) you can override the default mode (smart or not) by holding down SHIFT while you give the command.

If you set Smart Flip words when no index is open for work, the settings become the default ones that Cindex establishes for new indexes when you create them.

Acronyms

When a record field contains a phrase followed by text in parentheses (for example, an acronym or its expansion) you can exchange the contents of the leading text with the text in parentheses. Click anywhere in the field then click the toolbar button or hit CONTROL ALT S.

Cindex moves text smartly: if the leading phrase begins with a word from the Smart Flip set, that word is left untouched at the beginning of the field.

Names

When a record field contains a person's name, you can invert the surname and forname(s) by hitting CONTROL SHIFT I, or by choosing from the contextual menu (right click). Cindex will look for the first name beyond the cursor, and if it finds one will invert its current form.

If Cindex does not correctly find and invert the name, you can constrain it by selecting the text of the name before giving the command.
